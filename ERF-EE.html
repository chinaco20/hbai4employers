Weekly (EE) Contribution Form

note to self, using fund credit.

The Weekly 401k Employee Contribution Report form is for reporting voluntary 401k member contributions only. The single requirement is that the members must be at the 45% Apprentice level or higher. When a member reaches the 45% Apprentice level their 401k account under the carpenters plan is created. Voluntary contributions cannot be made until then, so deductions should not start until any retroactive step-up benefits are paid and posted.

The Employee 401K Contribution Worksheet form is in the Resources Library section here.

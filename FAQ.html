How do Liquidated Damages Waivers work?

When you start your account with us you are alloted 4 Monthly Liquidated Damages Waivers (Monthly LDW) and 4 Weekly Liquidated Damages Waivers (Weekly LDW) to be used on a revolving basis. To use an LDW the request must be made in letter form. The available LDW is then surrendered to us for 364 days starting from the pay period ending date (PPE) on the delinquency invoice. The hold is not afixed to the waiver request date nor the invoice date nor the invoice settle date. As an example, a Weekly LDW used on an invoice with a PPE of September 25, 2020 would be available for any new delinquency invoice with a PPE date of September 24, 2021 or later.

Per the delinquency policy, if your account is current, over the next 12 months you are allowed up to 4 monthly Liquidated Damages Waiver and 4 weekly Liquidated Damages Waiver for a twelve month period.
